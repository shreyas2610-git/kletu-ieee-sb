About Us

Where technology
meets community

The IEEE Student Branch at KLE Technological University is a dynamic hub for students passionate about technology, research, and innovation. Operating under the IEEE Bangalore Section and the North Karnataka SubSection, we bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering.

Our branch brings together students from diverse engineering disciplines, providing them access to IEEE's global network of professionals, cutting-edge publications, and technical resources. Through workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and collaborative projects, we cultivate the next generation of technology leaders.

With active societies spanning computer science, power electronics, robotics, and more, every student finds a community that matches their passion.

Global network

Access to IEEE's worldwide community of 400,000+ members across 160 countries.

Technical resources

IEEE Xplore, conferences, standards, and publications at your fingertips.

Peer community

Collaborate with like-minded students on projects, research, and competitions.
